Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 oz) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp black pepper
  • 4 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozarella
  • 3/4 lb Parmesan

Instruction

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic until browned. Stir in tomatoes and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel, Italian seasoning, 1tsp salt, pepper, and 2 tbsp parsley. Simmer for 1.5 hr.
  2. Boil salted water, and cook lasagna noodles for 8-10 mins. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, remaining parsley and 0.5 tsp salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 F.
  5. Assemble lasagna: 1.5 cup of meat sauce @ bottom. Arrange lasagna noodles, then top with ricotta cheese mixture, and then with Mozarella cheese. Add meat sauce, then parmesan, then repeat. Top with mozarella & parmesan.
  6. Cover w/ foil. Bake for 25 mins covered, then uncover and bake additional 25 mins.
