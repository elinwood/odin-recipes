Salmon Mentai

Ingredients

  • 120 gr salmon
  • 120 gr rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 0.5 tbsp mirin
  • 2 pinch salt
  • 0.5 tbsp sesame oil

Sauce

  • 1 tbsp tobiko
  • 2 tbsp kewpie
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • Nori

Instruction

  1. Slice salmon, marinade in soy sauce, salt and sesame oil.
  2. Add rice, top with Nori (shredded) into container
  3. Top rice with salmon
  4. Bake at 350 F until salmon is cooked (about 5-8 mins), internal temp 145 F
  5. Top with sauce. Broil at 450 F for 2-mins, until sauce browns.
  6. Take out and top with Tobiko
