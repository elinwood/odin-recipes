Spicy Pork

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb pork shoulder
  • 0.5 onion, sliced into thin strips
  • 3 stalks scallion, 2-inch pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 0.25 cup gochujang
  • 3 tbsp rice wine
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • sesame seed (garnish)

Instruction

  1. Slice pork into thin slices.
  2. Add all ingredients (except canola oil).
  3. Cover & marinate in fridge for 30 mins
  4. Heat oil in pan over high heat. Add pork.Stir fry pork in batches until slightly crispy on the edges (3-4 mins).
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds.
